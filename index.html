Academic Conference on

Qurān Translation in Malayalam

Methodology, Narration, Legacy

September, 04 2022

Department of Qurā'n and Related Sciences

in Collaboration with
Fath-hu rahman Centre of Excellence for Qurānic Studies[FCEQS]
Darul Hidaya Da'wa College, Manoor

Areas for submission

Qur’ān and Translation

  • Jurisprudence of Qur’ān translation
  • Debates on Qur’ān translation at global sphere
  • Qur’ān translation: merits and demerits
  • Studies on the translation of Qur’ān
  • Complications in translating process
  • Modern trends and theories in Qur’ān translation
  • Scope and limitation of Qur’ān translation
  • Oral and written mode of translation for Qur’ān
  • Linguistic barriers of Qur’ān translation
  • Cultural barriers of Qur’ān translation

Qur’ān Translations in Malayalam

  • Translations and commentary in Malayalam
  • History and legacy of translations
  • Methodology of Malayalam translations
  • Background of Malayalam translations
  • Factual and linguistic errors in Malayalam translations
  • Impact of Malayalam translations in the public sphere
  • Malayalam translations for Arabic Qur’ān exegesis
  • Prominent Keralite translators
  • Poetic composition in Malayalam translations
  • Arabic Malayalam translations of Qur’ān
  • Malayalam translations: a critical analysis
  • Researchers are encouraged to contribute high-quality original papers to this conference.
  • Paper should be on any topic related to the theme of the conference.
  • Paper must be unpublished and not presented in any seminar or conference.
  • Abstract and Paper must be submitted in the format of MS Word.
  • Authors will be asked to revise their papers according to the recommendations made by the review committee.
  • Abstracts and full papers should be written in English, Arabic or Malayalam

Important dates

  • 27 Jul 2022 : Abstract submission deadline
  • 30 Jul 2022 : Notification of abstract acceptance
  • 02 Sep 2022 : Deadline for Full paper submission
  • 04 Sep 2022 : Final Conference

The Department of Qur’ān and Related Sciences of Darul Hidaya Da’wa College, Manoor in collaboration with the Fath-hur rahman Centre of Excellence for Qur’ānic Studies (FCEQS), organizes an academic conference on “Qur’ān Translations in Malayalam: Methodology, Narration, and Legacy”.
